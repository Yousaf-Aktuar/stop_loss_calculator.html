Stop Loss (Aggregate XL)
Reinsurance Calculator

The stop loss treaty protects the cedent against an unfavourable total portfolio result over one year. Unlike per-risk or Cat XL, it responds to the aggregate loss S. The reinsurer pays RSL whenever S exceeds the retention a, up to the limit h. Above a + h the cedent bears the remaining loss alone.

Eq. (1.10): RSL = min( max(S − a, 0), h ) Bernegger / Fackler & Mack (2003)
